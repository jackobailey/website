I don't keep as many notes as I should. But those that I do keep, I tend to come back to again and again. So, in an attempt to cultivate better working habits, I'm making two changes to how I do my research. First, I am going to keep better notes. Second, I am going to make those notes available for others to use.

There's no real philosophy at work here. Any open notebook entries I write will probably be either half-formed thoughts or random scribbles. They will probably be a bit more technical than a normal blog post too. And, of course, whether anyone else actually reads them or finds them useful remains to be seen. Still, it does at least give you a window into my thinking and some of the research that I'm working on.

If you read a note and it triggers or thought or a question, please do let me know. I'm very happy to make time to chat, so feel free to send me [an email](mailto:jack.bailey@manchester.ac.uk).
